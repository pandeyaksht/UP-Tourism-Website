Travel Guide

Everything you need to know before exploring Uttar Pradesh.

Best Time to Visit

Summer

March–June
30°C – 45°C
Suitable for indoor attractions.

Monsoon

July–September
Pleasant greenery and waterfalls.

Winter

October–February
10°C – 25°C
Ideal for sightseeing.

Transportation

Airways

Major airports are located in Lucknow, Varanasi, Agra, Prayagraj and Ayodhya.

Railways

Well-connected railway network linking all major tourist destinations.

Roadways

National highways and UPSRTC buses connect every district.

Essential Travel Tips

Carry ID

Always keep a valid government ID while travelling.

Stay Hydrated

Carry drinking water during long trips.

Dress Comfortably

Wear comfortable clothing and footwear.

Photography

Respect photography restrictions at religious monuments.

Must Try Cuisine

Tunday Kababi

Famous Lucknow delicacy.

Mathura Peda

Traditional sweet of Uttar Pradesh.

Banarasi Chaat

Popular street food loved by tourists.

Emergency Contacts

Police

Dial 112 for immediate police assistance.

Ambulance

Dial 108 for emergency medical services.

Fire Brigade

Dial 101 in case of fire emergencies.

Tourist Helpline

1800-180-2877 (Toll Free)

Travel Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Carry valid identification.
  • Respect local traditions.
  • Keep monuments clean.
  • Book hotels in advance.
  • Wear comfortable footwear.

Don'ts

  • Do not litter tourist places.
  • Avoid entering restricted areas.
  • Do not damage monuments.
  • Avoid carrying excessive cash.
  • Respect photography restrictions.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to visit?

October to March offers pleasant weather and is ideal for sightseeing.
